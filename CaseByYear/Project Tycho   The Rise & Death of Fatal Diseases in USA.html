VIRUS CENTRAL

Measles

Measles is a very contagious (easily spread) infection that causes a rash all over your body

Hepatitis A

Hepatitis A is inflammation of the liver caused by the hepatitis A virus

Mumps

Mumps is a contagious viral infection that can cause painful swelling of the salivary glands,

Pertussis

Pertussis is a disease that causes very severe coughing that may last for months

Rubella

Rubella is a viral infection characterized by fever, headache and a distinctive red rash.
